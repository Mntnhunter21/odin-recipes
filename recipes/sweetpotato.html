Chipotle Sweet Potatoe Fries

This recipe will be a compliment to any hand food dish like burgers or ribs. The spicy seasoning and the natural sweetness of the potatoe contradict into a wonderful combination.

Ingredients

  • 2 sweet potatoes sliced thin
  • 3 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp chipotle chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp white ground pepper
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. In a large bowl toss all ingrediants together ot coat the sweet potatoes
  3. Place fries on baking sheet
  4. Bake for 15 minutes
